Ariel is an entrepreneur who has lived in San Francisco, Boston, Argentina, Spain, Vietnam, and Indonesia. He is currently the founder of Microverse, an online school for remote software developers with a mission to educate and connect 1 million people with remote jobs by 2030. Previously, Ariel was the co-founder and CEO of TouristEye, a travel startup that he grew to 1 million users and sold to Lonely Planet in 2013.
